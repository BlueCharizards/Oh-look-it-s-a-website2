Welcome!

The Milky Way Cafe is worlds away from any other cafes. It was created when two astronauts were stranded on the moon because their mission had technically been cancelled 0.3 seconds before launch. With nothing else to do and quickly depleting oxygen supplies, they decided that building a cafe was the most reasonable course of action. The cafe is built entirely out of moon rock, and ingredients are teleported with technology made by that one guy who actually lives on the moon.
